FAQ

Common questions

What is Floworax? +
Floworax is a Nigerian technology company building business tools for small businesses, freelancers, and service providers. Our first product, Flowora, helps you record sales, accept payments, track expenses, and manage bookings — all in one place.
Does it work without internet? +
Yes. Flowora Pay is built offline-first. You can record sales, view reports, and manage your store with no internet connection. Everything syncs automatically to the cloud when you reconnect. This means power cuts, bad network days, or travelling to areas with poor signal will never stop you from recording business activity.
How do payments work? +
Flowora uses Paystack to process payments. Your customers can pay by card, bank transfer, or USSD. Payments are verified automatically and settled directly to your bank account by Paystack — we never hold your money. Paystack typically settles funds within 24 hours on business days.
What does Paystack charge? +
Paystack charges 1.5% + ₦100 per transaction, capped at ₦2,000. This is Paystack's fee, not Floworax's. On the Free plan, Floworax does not add any additional transaction fees on top. On Pro and Business plans, there are no extra platform charges per transaction either.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit using SSL and at rest in our cloud database. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords. Your sales data is backed up automatically and you can export it to CSV anytime. We do not sell your data to third parties.
Can I use it on my phone? +
Yes. Flowora is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your Android or iPhone home screen directly from your browser — no app store required. It works like a native app, loads fast, and works offline.
Can I manage more than one business? +
Yes. Flowora supports multiple stores under one account. Each store has its own dashboard, sales history, and reports. This is useful if you run more than one business or manage a business with multiple locations.
Can churches and ministries use Flowora? +
Yes. Churches and ministries can use Flowora to collect offerings, track donations, manage bookings for events, and record expenses. Flowora Bookings (coming soon) will allow members to register and pay for events online.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Settings page. When you cancel, you keep access to your plan until the end of the billing period. After that, your account moves to the Free plan automatically. Your data is never deleted when you downgrade.
What happens to my data if I stop paying? +
Your data is never deleted if you stop paying. You move to the Free plan which allows up to 50 sales per month. All your historical data remains accessible. You can export everything to CSV at any time regardless of your plan.
When is Flowora Bookings coming? +
Flowora Bookings is currently in development. It will be available to Pro and Business plan users first. Join our waitlist below to be notified as soon as it launches and to get early access.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 14-day free trial on the Pro plan — no card required. If you subscribe and are unsatisfied within the first 7 days of a paid period, contact us at hello@floworax.com and we will review your request. Refunds are issued at our discretion based on usage.
How do I contact support? +
You can reach us at hello@floworax.com. Pro and Business plan users get priority support with faster response times. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.
